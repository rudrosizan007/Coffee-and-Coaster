Caramel Macchiato

There's no need to spend extra money to enjoy a warming, tasty cup of caramel macchiato. This delectable and frothy coffee treat, made famous by coffee spots around the world, is easy to make at home for a fraction of the price. With only four ingredients, you can try and make your own vanilla syrup and caramel sauce to have at hand for a daily dose of caramel macchiato heaven.

Caffe Latte

Caffe latte is a coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. The term comes from the Italian caffè e latte, caffelatte or caffellatte, which means "coffee and milk". In English, it is often shortened to just latte; either or both words sometimes have an accent on the final

Caffè Americano

Caffè Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, traditionally brewed coffee. The strength of an Americano varies with the number of shots of espresso and the amount of water added.
